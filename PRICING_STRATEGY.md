# Training Booking System - Pricing & Business Strategy

## Running Costs Analysis

### 1. Hosting & Infrastructure
- **Vercel/Netlify (Recommended)**: £20-100/month depending on usage
- **AWS/DigitalOcean**: £25-150/month for VPS + database
- **Database**: £10-50/month (PostgreSQL/MySQL managed service)
- **CDN & Assets**: £5-20/month

### 2. Third-party Services
- **Stripe**: 2.9% + 30p per transaction (major cost for payment processing)
- **SSL Certificates**: £0-50/year (often free with hosting)
- **Email Service** (Resend/SendGrid): £10-50/month
- **Domain**: £10-15/year

### 3. Maintenance & Updates
- **Your time**: 5-15 hours/month for updates, bug fixes, features
- **Security updates**: Ongoing (part of maintenance time)
- **Backup services**: £10-30/month

**Total Monthly Costs: £100-400/month** (excluding your time)

## Revenue Models & Pricing Strategy

### 1. SaaS Subscription Model (Recommended)

#### Starter: £99/month
- Up to 50 bookings/month
- Basic features
- Email support

#### Professional: £299/month  
- Up to 200 bookings/month
- Advanced booking features
- Phone support
- Custom branding

#### Enterprise: £599/month
- Unlimited bookings
- Multi-location support
- API access
- Priority support

### 2. Transaction-based Pricing
- **3-5% per booking** (on top of Stripe fees)
- For ACME's £650 course = £19.50-32.50 per booking
- More scalable but harder to predict revenue

### 3. Setup + Monthly Model
- **One-time setup**: £2,000-5,000
- **Monthly maintenance**: £150-400/month
- Good for custom implementations

## Value Proposition for Training Companies

### Immediate Value
- **Time savings**: 10-20 hours/week on manual booking management
- **Reduced no-shows**: Automated reminders and confirmations
- **Professional image**: Modern, mobile-friendly booking system
- **Payment automation**: Instant payments vs. chasing invoices

### Financial Benefits for Clients
- **Increased bookings**: 24/7 online booking vs. phone-only
- **Higher conversion**: Easier booking process
- **Reduced admin costs**: Less manual work = lower labor costs
- **Deposit collection**: Immediate deposits reduce cancellations

## Market Analysis for Training Companies

### Target Market Size
- Gas training companies: 200-500 in UK
- Heat pump training: Growing market (100+ companies)
- OFTEC training: 50-100 providers
- **Realistic target**: 20-50 clients in 2-3 years

### Client Value Calculation
For a company like ACME doing 50 bookings/month at £650 average:
- **Monthly revenue**: £32,500
- **Your software value**: Saves 15 hours admin @ £25/hour = £375/month
- **Increased bookings**: Even 10% more = £3,250/month additional revenue
- **Justifiable cost**: £300-500/month easily pays for itself
